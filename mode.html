Modes of sustainable and green transportation

The current transport modes require enormous amounts of energy to run vehicles. They emit harmful gases causing environmental pollution and lead to health complications. In order to eliminate these harmful implications it is useful to utilize eco-friendly modes of transport.


The following are some modes of green transport available in this age [Citation8]:

  1. Bicycle:

    2. Riding a bicycle eliminates producing greenhouse gases.

  2. Electric bike:

    3. They don't produce harmful emissions. They are powered by batteries and they do not produce any emissions.

  3. Electric vehicles:

    4. They are powered totally by electricity and do not emit any dangerous gases. The power can be tapped from renewable technologies.

  4. Green trains:

    5. They are innovative green technology; they do not emit harmful gases.

  5. Multiple occupant vehicles:

    6. A number of people sharing the same car, it reduces the number of cars on the roads network and minimize the environmental pollution.

  6. Service and freight vehicles:

    7. They utilize electricity and biofuels instead of using regular fossil fuel sources.

  7. Hybrid cars:

    8. They have two powered engines; one runs on gasoline and the other is an electric motor. They conserve energy and reduce fuel consumption.

  8. Pedestrians:

    9. Walking to work, school and to shops does not emit any greenhouse gas. It does not cost any expenses and walking is good for the human health.
