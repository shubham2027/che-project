SOME SUGGESTIVE METHODS OF GREEN TRANSPORTATION FOR THE PEOPLE WHICH DOESN'T HARM ENVIRONMENT



  1. Prt

    2. Personal Rapid Transit (PRT) Pod Cars are small, automated, and driverless vehicles designed for point-to-point transportation within urban environments. Operating on elevated tracks, these electric pods offer a sustainable and efficient alternative to traditional public transportation. PRT systems aim to reduce congestion, enhance mobility, and provide a personalized, on-demand transit experience for users.

  2. ISRO, Tata Motors' fuel cell bus

    3. ISRO (Indian Space Research Organisation) and Tata Motors collaborated on a hydrogen fuel cell bus, showcasing India's advancements in clean transportation. The fuel cell technology aims to reduce emissions, utilizing hydrogen as a sustainable energy source. This partnership exemplifies the integration of space and automotive expertise to drive innovation in eco-friendly mobility solutions for the future.

  3. Delhi Metro

    4. Delhi Metro, India's capital city rapid transit system, is a modern, efficient, and eco-friendly mode of transportation. Serving Delhi and its satellite cities, the metro network has significantly eased congestion, reducing travel time and environmental impact. With its extensive reach and advanced technology, Delhi Metro continues to be a vital component of the region's sustainable urban mobility infrastructure.

  4. CNG-Electric Hybrid Bus

    5. CNG-electric hybrid buses combine compressed natural gas (CNG) and electric power for a cleaner, more sustainable urban transit solution. These buses reduce greenhouse gas emissions and fuel costs by utilizing a combination of CNG and electric propulsion. With dual-mode functionality, they offer operational flexibility, contributing to environmentally conscious public transportation systems in urban areas.

  5. Mahindra's zero-emission e2o

    6. Mahindra's e2o is a compact, zero-emission electric car designed for urban commuting. With a small footprint and a fully electric drivetrain, it provides a sustainable and cost-effective solution for short-distance travel. The e2o's eco-friendly features contribute to reduced air pollution and promote the adoption of electric vehicles in the urban mobility landscape.

  6. E-rickshaws

    7. The e-rickshaw scheme has been promoted quite actively by Delhi government as an environmentally friendly alternative.E-rickshaws, or electric rickshaws, are three-wheeled vehicles powered by electric motors. They provide an eco-friendly and economical mode of short-distance transportation in urban areas. With zero emissions and low operating costs, e-rickshaws contribute to sustainable mobility solutions. These compact vehicles have gained popularity in many countries as a clean and affordable alternative to traditional rickshaws or auto-rickshaws.
